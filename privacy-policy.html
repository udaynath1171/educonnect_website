1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information:

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize the EduConnect service
  • Facilitate communication between schools, teachers, and parents
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share your information:

  • With your school or educational institution (if they onboarded you)
  • With service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under confidentiality agreements
  • When legally required (e.g., court order, legal process)

11. Role-Specific Privacy Notes

We recognize the unique data privacy needs of different users on EduConnect:

  • Parents: We collect limited personal data required to link you with your child's school and provide you timely updates on their progress.
  • Teachers: Your name, contact details (as shared by your school), and interactions with parents are used solely to support teaching and communication.
  • School Administrators: We may collect information such as your contact details and administrative decisions for managing school-wide communication.
  • Students: Personal data about students (e.g., name, class, performance) is entered by school staff or parents and is not shared with third parties. We operate in compliance with children's data protection laws, and student data is only visible to authorized users.

12. Legal Compliance

EduConnect complies with the following applicable laws in Mauritius:

  • Data Protection Act 2017 - governing the collection, use, and rights related to personal data.
  • ICT Act 2001 - regulating responsible use of technology and cybersecurity.
  • Child Protection Act - safeguarding student data and minors from exploitation.
  • Copyright Act 2014 - ensuring intellectual property rights are respected in our app and content.

Where applicable, we also follow principles of international standards like the EU GDPR to ensure data privacy and user protection.
